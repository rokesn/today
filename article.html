What is LiveBall training?

LiveBall is a revolutionary tennis training system that combines high-intensity cardio workouts with competitive doubles tennis. Each 45-minute session burns 600-800+ calories while improving tennis skills, cardiovascular fitness, and social connections.

No tennis experience is required! LiveBall is specifically designed for all skill levels - from complete beginners to advanced players. You'll need tennis shoes (proper court shoes are recommended), comfortable athletic clothing, and a water bottle.
